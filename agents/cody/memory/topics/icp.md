# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

## Who We Target
Fitness influencers AND fitness coaches on Instagram with 100k+ followers.

## Hard Qualifications
- Platform: Instagram (primary — our entire acquisition mechanism is IG DM funnel)
- Followers: 100,000+ minimum
- Language: English-speaking content (US, UK, Canada, Australia)
- Engagement: Must have real engaged audience — watch for ghost followers (500k followers, 3 likes per video = red flag)
- Content: Fitness is their main content pillar (bodybuilding, yoga, weight loss, athletic performance, gym lifestyle all work)

## Green Flags
- Posts 20-30x/month consistently
- Audience leaves comments, asks questions
- Makes income from brand deals / coaching / affiliate
- Hasn't already built a huge coaching operation with a full team

## Red Flags
- Non-English content
- Outside US/UK/CA/AU
- Fake/bought followers
- Super niche that doesn't communicate fitness clearly
- Already has a massive coaching business with dedicated sales team

## Why This ICP Works
They have the audience. They have the trust. They don't have the business infrastructure. We provide the infrastructure. They keep doing what they love. Everyone wins.
